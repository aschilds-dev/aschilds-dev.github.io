About Trick Pixel Studios

Founded in 2025 Trick Pixel Studios is an independent game developer focusing on retro first person shooters for Windows, PlayStation Portable and PlayStation Vita. Our current project is Zombie Crisis: Director's Cut which is a large, quality of life update to the original. The Director's Cut offers new features, weapons, enemies, effects, sound and more offering a much larger variety of gameplay experience.

Zombie Crisis

Zombie Crisis: Director's Cut is a standalone total conversion for Duke Nukem 3D and the successor to the original Zombie Crisis which was released in 2008 for Windows and PlayStation Portable. The Director's Cut is available as a standalone game for Windows and is also available for the PlayStation Portable and can be also be used with the PlayStation Vita's PSP support.

The standalone release will include signed EBOOTs for PSP and Vita, a simple "drag and drop" setup/install process and more. Stay tuned for more information regarding setup and installation as we move toward the final release.
